How to use

When viewing the list:

  • To open a note in the reader, press it once;
  • To open a note in the editor, press it twice;
  • To delete a note, press the "🗑" on the list item;
  • To open a new note in the editor, press "＋";
  • To delete all notes, press "∅";
  • To view the user guide, press "❔".

When using the editor:

  • To save the content to a note, press "🖫";
  • To close the editor, press "✖", and if changes are unsaved, a prompt will appear asking whether or not to save before closing;
  • To delete the open note, press "🗑".

When using the reader:

  • To see information about the note, press "🛈";
  • To open the note in the editor, press "✎";
  • To exist the editor, press "◀".

General information

  • The notes are stored locally. This makes them available at a later time.
  • This comes with a storage limit. After this limit is reached, optimal functionality is not guaranteed.

Please note!

  • It it not recommended to store any kind of personal information here, as its safety and security cannot be guaranteed. Use with care!
